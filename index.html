Dr. Kezhi Wang

Senior Lecturer
Department of Computer and Information Sciences
Northumbria University, Newcastle upon Tyne, NE1 8ST, United Kingdom
Email Kezhi

Dr Kezhi Wang received his B.E. and M.E. degrees in School of Automation from Chongqing University, China, in 2008 and 2011, respectively. He received his Ph.D. degree in Engineering from the University of Warwick, U.K. in 2015. He was a Senior Research Officer in University of Essex, U.K. Currently he is a Senior Lecturer in Department of Computer and Information Sciences at Northumbria University, U.K.
